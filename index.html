Signal Flow
1
CoinGecko — BTC Price
Free public API fetches Bitcoin price in USD and 24h % change. No API key required.
2
Alternative.me — Fear & Greed Index
Free public API returns the crypto Fear & Greed Index (0–100) based on volatility, volume, social sentiment, surveys, and dominance.
3
Vercel AI Function — Signal Computation
Combines BTC price change and Fear & Greed into a stress score. Maps score to burnRateBps, apyMultiplier, and mintCap using AI logic tuned for NMNT.
4
Gelato — On-Chain Push
Automated task calls pushSignal() on the AIOracle contract every 4 hours. The contract validates, clamps, then updates NMNTToken + NMNTStaking atomically.
5
NMNT Token & Staking Update
All 100M+ token holders instantly get the new burn rate on their next transfer. Stakers see updated APY in real time.

Why Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is the most liquid, most watched, most predictive signal in crypto. When BTC moves, everything moves. NeuralMint is the first token to make this its core mechanic.

Bear Market Protection
When BTC crashes and fear spikes, NMNT automatically increases its burn rate (deflationary pressure) and boosts staking APY to reward holders who don't panic sell.
Bull Run Mechanics
In periods of extreme BTC greed, burn rate drops to near zero and more tokens can be minted — giving the ecosystem room to grow alongside the overall market rally.
AI Signal Logic
The oracle combines two free data sources: CoinGecko (BTC price + 24h change) and Alternative.me (Fear & Greed Index). These are combined into a stress score that maps to tokenomics parameters.
Fully On-Chain
Every parameter change is a blockchain transaction. Transparent, auditable, and verifiable on Polygonscan. The Gelato automation log shows every oracle update.
